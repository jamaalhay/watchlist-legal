Support

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Need help with PikPal? You're in the right place. PikPal is built by an independent developer, and we read every message. The fastest way to reach us is by email — we aim to respond within 2 business days.

Contact Us

For any questions, problems, or help with your account, email us at:

Email: support@pikpal.app

Report Content or a User

PikPal has zero tolerance for objectionable content and abusive behavior. You can report or block directly in the app:

  • In the app: tap the ••• menu on any review, take, stamp, comment, or user profile to Report the content or Block the user.
  • Blocked users' content is hidden from you immediately.
  • We review reports and act on violations within 24 hours.

For urgent safety concerns, email support@pikpal.app with the details.

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from inside the app:

  • Open Settings → Delete Account and confirm.
  • This permanently removes your profile, rankings, reviews, stamps, watchlist, and friend connections. It cannot be undone.

If you can't access the app, email support@pikpal.app from the address or phone number on your account and we'll help.

Privacy & Data Requests

For privacy-specific requests — accessing, correcting, or exporting your data — see our Privacy Policy or email privacy@pikpal.app.

Feedback & Bug Reports

Have an idea or hit a bug? Use the feedback button on the main screens of the app, or email support@pikpal.app. We genuinely read it all.
